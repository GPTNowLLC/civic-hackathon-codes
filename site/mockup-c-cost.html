What this tells the City

Citation quality is not an academic metric. Each missing or wrong section number maps onto a specific dollar amount on a Portlander’s ledger and a specific number of weeks taken from their life. The gap between “Out-of-Box” and “Enriched” is roughly 18× in worst-case dollars and 17× in worst-case weeks.

The cheapest investment the City can make is the prompt-side fix — re-writing the resident’s question before it ever reaches the model. That alone moves the answer from the red zone toward the green.
