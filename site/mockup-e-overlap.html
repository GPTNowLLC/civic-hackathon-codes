How to read the shape

The green-only field is the largest — that volume is what enrichment uniquely added: sequenced steps, THOW disambiguation, dollar ranges.

The red-only field is thin and contains the only wrong answers — what the AI uniquely had to say without data was either vague or incorrect.

The amber-and-green overlap holds the bulk of correct, citation-grade zoning facts. Data access is what unlocks correctness.
